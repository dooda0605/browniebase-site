dududada ("we", "us", or "operator") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information while providing Salpim ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information – Email address (optional, only when you link an account)
  • Device Identifier – Anonymous user ID (automatically generated on first launch)
  • App Settings – Display language, country/region, personal filters (allergies, dietary restrictions, etc.)
  • Scan History – Ingredient analysis results and saved ingredient lists (original images are NOT stored on our servers)
  • Subscription Information – Purchase confirmation from Google Play, subscription start and expiry dates (we do NOT collect payment card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Ingredient label analysis and AI-powered explanations
  • Multilingual translation and language preference management
  • Library (favorites) ingredient synchronization
  • Personalized services such as daily ingredient summaries
  • Pro subscription management and access verification
  • Service error analysis and quality improvement

3. Data Retention

Your data is deleted immediately upon account deletion or withdrawal request. Where required by applicable law, certain data may be retained for the legally mandated period before being securely destroyed.

4. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase Inc. – Database and authentication (US servers, Privacy Policy)
  • Google ML Kit – On-device text recognition (OCR). Images are NOT sent to Google servers.
  • Google Play – Subscription payment processing. Payment card details are managed solely by Google; we only receive purchase confirmation (subscription status and expiry date). (Google Privacy Policy)

5. Camera & Image Handling

The App requests camera and gallery access to photograph ingredient labels. Text recognition (OCR) is processed on-device. Original images are never transmitted to or stored on external servers.

6. Security Measures

  • All network communications are encrypted via HTTPS (TLS)
  • Authentication tokens are stored in secure on-device storage
  • Passwords are one-way hashed (not reversible)

7. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access and correct your personal information
  • Delete your account (App → My Page → Delete Account)
  • Request restriction of processing

Upon account deletion, all data stored on Supabase servers is removed immediately.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 14 years of age. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child under 14, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as required by law or service changes. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notice or this page.
